Benivegna Building Company Inc.
The foundation for Benivegna Building Company began in 1951 when Tony Benivegna received one of Michigan's first building licenses. Since that time, The Benivegna tradition has become synonymous with fine craftsmanship and original design.

Today thousands of homes bear the Benivegna signature. The unique combination of Old-World artistry and today's most progressive ideas in architecture have been carried on by Ron Benivegna, the son of Tony and Alberta.
